Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a book? +
To upload a book, you need to create an account and log in. Then go to the Dashboard and click "Upload a Book". Fill in the book details and select your file (PDF or EPUB format).
What file formats are supported? +
We currently support PDF and EPUB formats. The maximum file size is 50MB per book.
Is BookFinder free to use? +
Yes! BookFinder is completely free to use. You can upload, search, and download books without any cost.
How can I delete my uploaded books? +
Go to "My Books" section in your dashboard. Each book has a delete button that allows you to remove it from the platform.
